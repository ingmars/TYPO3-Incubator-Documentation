TYPO3 Extension Developer
=========================

Like the TYPO3 Core, extensions must also be aware of accessibility, especially
in regard to frontend output. There are a variety of types of output to
consider, including forms, multimedia, navigation, and data tables.

All these types of output have their own demands regarding accessibility. To meet
the WCAG 2.0 guidelines, some TYPO3 specific issues have to be taken into account.
